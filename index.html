She is the light that brightens up every corner of my world, and I am that "taara" that reflects her warmth in quiet devotion.

Our Story

I don’t know how to explain her. She’s not someone I just met—she's someone I somehow always knew. It feels like life brought her when I had almost forgotten how to feel again. She didn’t come into my life with noise—she came in with calm. The kind of calm that makes you want to slow down and just sit with your thoughts. She laughs like nothing’s broken and listens like everything matters. And when I look at her, I feel like I’m seeing a version of life I thought I didn’t deserve—soft, pure, and maybe even a little magical. Maybe this is what comfort looks like in human form. She doesn’t try to fix me, she just stands beside me—and somehow, that’s more than enough.
